Ch. 11 Supplements

Requirements

  • The Qal Wayyiqtol paradigms of קטל and בּנה must also be memorized.
  • The קטל Wayyiqtol form is very easy if you know the yiqtol form. Just add waw + pathach + dagesh forte.
  • The בּנה paradigm is special. The 3ms, 3fs, and 2ms drop the final Hey and cause accent shifts as well as vowel changes. A lot of students forgot these changes in the past!
  • You may download the Qal Wayyiqtol Strong and III-Hey verb Paradigms Practice Sheet for practice.
