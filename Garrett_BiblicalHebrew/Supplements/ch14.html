Ch. 14 Supplements

Requirements

  • In chapter 14, tables 14.2 and 14.4 must be memorized. You will be asked to reproduce the paradigms in the exam.
  • You only need to be able to recognize other forms.

Mnemonics

  • Kaf indicates the 2nd person:
    • סוּסְךָ
    • סוּסֵךְ
    • סוּסְכֶם
    • סוּסְכֶן
    • סוּסֶ֫יךָ
    • סוּסַ֫יִךְ
    • סוּסֵיכֶם
    • סוּסֵיכֶן
  • Hey indicates the 3rd person:
    • סוּסָהּ
    • סוּסֶ֫יהָ
    • סוּסֵיהֶם
    • סוּסֵיהֶן
  • You will learn in ch. 17 that the 3rd person subject pronouns also begins with Hey: הוּא, היא, הֵם , הֵן.
