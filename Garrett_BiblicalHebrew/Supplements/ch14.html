Ch. 14 Supplements

Requirements

  • In chapter 14, tables 14.2 and 14.4 must be memorized. You will be asked to reproduce the paradigms in the exam.
  • You only need to be able to recognize other forms.
  • Starting with quiz 14, there will be about 2 sentence translations for each quiz. You will need to type your translations in the boxes. Your OTA will grade these translations. There will also be verb parsing questions. The verbs for passing are taken from the sentence translation questions.

Mnemonics

  • Kaf indicates the 2nd person:
    • סוּסְךָ
    • סוּסֵךְ
    • סוּסְכֶם
    • סוּסְכֶן
    • סוּסֶ֫יךָ
    • סוּסַ֫יִךְ
    • סוּסֵיכֶם
    • סוּסֵיכֶן
  • Hey indicates the 3rd person:
    • סוּסָהּ
    • סוּסֶ֫יהָ
    • סוּסֵיהֶם
    • סוּסֵיהֶן
  • You will learn in ch. 17 that the 3rd person subject pronouns also begins with Hey: הוּא, הִיא, הֵם , הֵן.
